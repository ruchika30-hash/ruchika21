About Lochquarry Centre

Lochquarry Outdoor Centre is set in achres of land in the heart of the majestic Argyll Hills.On our doorstep is not only magnificent scenery,but also a breath-taking selection of outdoor and adventurous activities. With activities designed to meet the needs of all ages and experiences of young people. Lochquarry truly brings adventure to everyone.
